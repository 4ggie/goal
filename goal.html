Goal

A goal is an idea of the future or desired result that a person or a group of people envision, plan and commit to achieve.
People endeavour to reach goals within a finite time by setting deadlines.

A goal is roughly similar to a purpose or aim, the anticipated result which guides reaction,
or an end, which is an object, either a physical object or an abstract object, that has intrinsic value.
